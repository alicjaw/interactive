THE MASTER



                                                                                   outside    old calculations

                                                                                                  where the maneuver with age                 forgotten

                                                                                                                  arisen inferring





                                                                                                  long ago he grasped the helm of that

                                                                                                                                                                                conflagration

                                                                                                                                                                                at his feet





                                                                                                  of the unanimous horizon

                                                                                                                                                    there is preparing

                                                                                                                                                                      stirring and mixing





                                                                                                  in the fist that might clutch it

                                                                                                                                   as one menaces a destiny




                                                                                                                                                                    and the winds





                                                                                                  the unique

                                                                                                                                  Number

                                                                                                                                                                which cannot be another
