solitary   distraught   feather


save that a midnight toque encounters   or   grazes it


        and      immobilizes


in velvet crumpled by a fit of    dark    laughter


        this   rigid   whiteness


pathetic


        in   opposition   to the sky


too much


        not to mark


exiguously


        whosoever


bitter   prince of the reef


        makes a headdress of it in heroic style


irresistible but contained


        by his   small   reason virile


                                                                        as a flash
