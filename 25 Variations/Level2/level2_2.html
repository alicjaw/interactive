BE IT

             that the abyss

              blanched unbound furious

             under an incline desperately hangs

             on wing its own in advance

             of an ill-staged flight

             fallen back and covering the eruptions

  •              cutting the surges

  •              most inwardly resumes

  •               the shadow buried deep

  •              by this alternate sail

    •              even adapting to the wingspan

                 its yawning depth as great as the hull of a vessel

                 listed to one or the other side
