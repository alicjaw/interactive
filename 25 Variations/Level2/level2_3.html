BE IT

                                                                                              that the Abyss





                                                                                                              blanched

                                                                                                              unbound

                                                                                                              furious





                                                                                   under an                                 incline

                                                                desperately hangs

                                                                                                                                                on wing its own

                                                                                                              in                                                               advance

                                                                                                              of

                                                                                                              an





                                                                      ill-staged                         flight

                                                                                                              fallen

                                                                                                              back

                                                                                                              and                                covering the eruptions





                                                 cutting short

                                                                                  the surges

                                                                                                              most

                                                                                                              inwardly

                                                                                                              resumes





                                                                                                                                the shadow buried

                                                                                                                                in

                                                                                                                                the







                                                                                                                                deep

                                                                                                                                                                by this alternate sail





                                                                                                                                even

                                                                                                                                adapting

                                                                                                                                to                                                    the wingspan

                                                                              its

                                                                                              yawning depth

                                                                                                                  as great as the hull of a vessel

                                                                                                                                                                listed to one

                                                                                                                                  or the other side
